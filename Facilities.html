FACILITIES

COMPUTER LAB

Computer education is imparted to students from all classes I to IX. A Power-point presentation facility enhances the task at hand for the teacher & the taught. The curriculum is so organized so as to familiarize the students with MS office,Synfig Studio, Flash, Java Script, Java Programming, Internet, etc.

SPORTS FACILITIES

K.L.F.E.S. school offers a unique sports ground for overall development and physical fitness. A state – of art sports center offers facilities such as skating, basketball ground, volleyball ground, cricket ground, Kho - Kho ground, football and hockey ground and Indoor facilities like badminton, Table Tennis, chess and Carom.

SCIENCE LABORATORIES

Fully equipped and totally updated separate laboratories for Physics, Chemistry and Biology cater to the Practical Education. The other highlight of each laboratory is a dedicated space for sit-down learning aided by presentation facilities.

COUNSELING CENTER

Dedicated weekly counseling classes on various aspects of Personality Development and Inter-Personal Skill. Seminars to en-map the career choices of the students. Remedial counseling for students facing problems in their academic, social, or personal spheres.

LIBRARY

A well – designed & equipped Library, aims at providing curricular & extra- curricular reading opportunities that would stimulate the students with information, knowledge & insight into various aspects of life as well as different facets about their sought after career. A dedicated period per week to all the classes serves the purpose.
